There are several systems that work together to create these clips.

  • One polls Twitch's streams API every couple minutes to determine the most popular streamers who are currently live.
  • A second joins all the chat rooms of those streamers.
  • Another aggregates the chat messages from those chat rooms to detect spikes in the number of chat messages.
  • These spikes are then sent to the final service to capture the moment via Twitch's clips API.
